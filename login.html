Leave management

Employees can request and manage different types of leaves, such as vacation, sick leave, or personal time off, through the software. Managers can review and approve or deny leave requests, and the system keeps track of the available leave balances.

Attendance processing

Employees can log their working hours, breaks, and absences using various methods, such as biometric devices, swipe cards, or mobile applications. The software records this information and calculates total working hours.

Payroll integration

Attendance management software often integrates with payroll systems, enabling seamless transfer of attendance data to calculate salaries, overtime, and other related compensation accurately.
